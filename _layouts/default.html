Appeals

If you would like to appeal a punishment please send an email to the address below, including your Minecraft in-game name, the reason for which you were punished, why you believe the punishment is incorrect or too severe and any counter-evidence you have. We will deal with your appeal as quickly as possible. Thank you for your patience.
