About us

Founded in early 2017, the Stratus Network is the home for the PGM (PvP Game Manager) plugin, which was originally made for the now closed Overcast Network. With PGM and other custom-coded plugins, Stratus offers both competitive and casual team-based PvP and, most importantly, a dedicated and awesome community.
